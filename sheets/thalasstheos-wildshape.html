Thalasstheos — Wild Shape Codex

Water Genasi · Druid 5 (Circle of the Land — Coast) — her shapes, her rules, and the Water Form

How Your Wild Shape Works

Transform: an Action (you're not a Circle of the Moon druid). You have 2 uses, regained on a short or long rest.

What you can become: a Beast you have seen before. At your level the cap is CR ½ or lower, with a swim speed allowed but NO flying speed — flying forms (your "bird") unlock at druid level 8. (At level 8: CR 1 and flight.)

How long: hours equal to half your druid level — 2 hours at level 5, 3 at level 6. Revert as a bonus action, or automatically when the form drops to 0 HP (leftover damage carries to your true body).

In a form: you use the beast's physical stats & HP (your own HP is set aside); you keep your INT, WIS, CHA, personality, and memories. You can't cast spells, but you can keep concentration going. Your gear melds away.

The exception is your Water Form — by your Pure Water Genasi gift it can heal itself like a Moon druid (see below), making it your durable "tank" shape.

★ The Water Form (your signature shape)

The Water Form — “Tideshape”

Medium beast (Wild Shape · homebrew), amphibious — a living, amorphous body of seawater
AC 13  ·  HP 22  ·  Speed 30 ft, swim 40 ft
STR 14 (+2)DEX 14 (+2)CON 14 (+2) (you keep your own WIS / INT / CHA)

Amphibious. You breathe air and water.

Amorphous. You can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch without squeezing, and flow through other creatures' spaces (their space is difficult terrain for you).

Watery Body. Resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing; immunity to poison and the poisoned condition; you can't be grappled or restrained (you simply flow free). Vulnerable to cold damage — cold can freeze you solid.

Tidal Mending. (Like a Circle of the Moon druid.) As a bonus action, spend a spell slot to regain 1d8 HP per level of the slot while in this form.

Actions

Surge (slam). Melee, +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 1d8 + 2 bludgeoning, and you may push the target 5 ft (a breaking wave).

Douse. Sweep over and extinguish nonmagical fire in a 5-ft space, or smother the flames on a burning creature.

Revert with a bonus action, or automatically at 0 HP. The self-heal makes this your front-line / survival shape; everything else below is for scouting, swimming, and escape.

How to use it: drop into the Water Form when you need to soak hits or hold a line (it heals itself); become the Octopus or Constrictor Snake to slip a trap, scout, or escape underwater; the Reef Shark or Black Bear when you need a sturdier body in a pinch. Remember: in any form you can't cast spells — so shape up when it's time to move, hide, or tank, and shift back to heal and control.
