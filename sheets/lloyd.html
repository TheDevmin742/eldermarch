Backstory
A man born to an aristocratic family — so ugly he was deemed an orc. He watched his house fall into ruinous debt, sunk by bad investments and the manipulation of corrupt nobles, and watched himself rot along with it… until one day he hit his head, and his whole personality changed. He turned his life around, and is now dedicated to earning money — by civil and decidedly unsavory means alike — to drag his parents' estate back to glory. Somewhere in the turn, he took up a paladin's oath, swearing (loosely) by Aurys, the Gilded Hand — god of honest coin, fair weights, and kept oaths; whether that god is listening, or even real, no one can say.

Additional Features & Notes
Silver tongue, iron shovel. Talks his way in, robs them blind, and brawls his way out — all four Charisma skills maxed (+7).
The wealth-god oath. A paladin sworn to Aurys — god of coin, fair weights, and kept oaths — whom he only half-believes in. His greed and his vow pull against each other, and the spade shines only for an oath kept.
Debt at his heels. Collectors hunt him; coin is both his ideal and his leash — easy hooks for side trouble.
