Backstory
Born high in the Stormy Mountains to Wrayne (mother) and Errk (father), in a small aarakocran flock. A bright, useless boy and a not-very-good thief, he lived there until he was seventeen — when war came down out of the higher peaks and tore the flock apart. Kree fled, and has never gone back. Seven years of inns, taverns, and the backs of carts followed, drifting wherever the wind or a coin led, telling everyone he didn't care for towns or the people in them. Then it rained out of season in Brindlewick, he went down to see why, and found a new flock he refuses to fly from. The mountains ahead are the ones he left — and he's going up anyway.

The whispering sword. Has attuned-in-spirit only once, mid-battle, for a single strike — the blade tasted a soul and wants more. Kree keeps it sheathed and keeps the watch.
Hooks. Ember foretold a "reunion." Old Riggs at the Wayfarers' Camp has a lead on where the scattered flock flew. The mountain ahead is home.
