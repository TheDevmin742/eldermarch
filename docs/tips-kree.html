✦Weapons

  • Shortbow +5 · 1d6+2 · 80/320 — Your safe ranged option — from range, never point-blank (disadvantage).
  • Rapier / Black Sword (Riftshard) +5 · 1d6–1d8+2 · finesse — For diving in beside an ally; the Black Sword works like a shortsword.
  • Dagger +5 · 1d4+2 · thrown 20/60 — A backup blade you can throw.
  • Talons +5 · 1d4 slashing — A natural weapon even with your hands full.

✦Sneak Attack — your main damage

  • +3d6, once per turn — Add it when you hit with a finesse/ranged weapon AND you have advantage, OR an ally is within 5 ft of the target (and you don't have disadvantage). Set this up every turn.

✦Actions & Abilities

  • Cunning Action Bonus action — Dash, Disengage, or Hide — Disengage lets you leave melee with no opportunity attack.
  • Steady Aim Bonus action — Advantage on your next attack (your Speed becomes 0 this turn) → guaranteed Sneak Attack with the bow.
  • Uncanny Dodge Reaction — Halve the damage of one attack that hits you.
  • Scout: Skirmisher Reaction — When a foe ends its turn within 5 ft, move half your speed without provoking.
  • Flight Aarakocra — Fly 50 ft (not in medium/heavy armor) — take the high ground; melee can't follow.
  • Expertise — Double proficiency on Stealth, Perception (and Nature, Survival from Scout).

✦Items

  • Potion of Healing ×2 Action — Regain 2d4+2 HP. Also a Fire-Resistance potion (resist fire 1 hr).
  • The Red Amulet attuned — Quiet now — the relic that drank the manor's fire.

✦General Combat Rules — the ones that trip people up

  • Opportunity attacks — Leaving a hostile creature's reach (usually 5 ft) on your turn lets it make one free melee attack. Avoid it: Disengage, teleport, fly clear, or simply don't leave. Standing up or moving within its reach is fine.
  • Ranged attack in melee = disadvantage — Firing a bow, thrown weapon, or ranged spell attack while an enemy is within 5 ft of you (and can see you) is at disadvantage. Step away first or switch to melee.
  • Spell range & areas — A spell only works within its listed range, and you usually must SEE the target. Area spells (cube, sphere, line, cone) hit everyone inside — allies included — unless the spell says otherwise.
  • Concentration — You can hold only ONE concentration spell at a time; a second ends the first. Take damage and you make a CON save (DC 10 or half the damage, whichever's higher) to keep it.
  • Advantage / disadvantage — Roll 2d20, take the higher (adv) or lower (disadv). They don't stack — any advantage plus any disadvantage cancel to a straight roll.
  • Cover — Half cover = +2 AC & Dex saves; three-quarters = +5; total cover = can't be targeted directly.
  • Down at 0 HP — Unconscious; roll a death save each turn (10+ = success). 3 successes = stable, 3 fails = dead. Nat 20 = up at 1 HP. Any healing wakes you. Being hit at 0 = a failed save (a crit = two).

✦Common Actions anyone can take

  • Attack — One attack (more with Extra Attack). Shove & Grapple are options of this action.
  • Dash — Double your movement this turn.
  • Disengage — Your movement won't provoke opportunity attacks this turn.
  • Dodge — Attacks against you have disadvantage; you make Dex saves with advantage (until your next turn). Use it when low or holding ground.
  • Help — Give an ally advantage — on a check, or on an attack against a creature within 5 ft of you.
  • Hide — Stealth check to become hidden (needs cover/obscurement); attacking from hiding has advantage.
  • Ready — Set a trigger now (“when X happens, I do Y”) and spend your reaction to do it.
  • Search — Actively look for something (Perception or Investigation).
  • Shove attack option — Athletics vs their Athletics/Acrobatics: push 5 ft or knock prone.
  • Grapple attack option — Athletics contest: target's Speed becomes 0.
  • Improvise a weapon — Hit with a chair/rock/torch: usually 1d4 + your mod (or use a similar weapon's stats).
  • Use an Object — Pull a lever, drink a potion, throw oil or holy water.
