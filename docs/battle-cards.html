How to Win Your Turn

Every turn, every character — and the rookie mistakes that cost fights

✦Each of your turns you get ALL of these

  • Move — Up to your Speed; you may split it (move, act, move again).
  • One Action — The big thing: Attack, cast a spell, Dash, Dodge, Hide, Help…
  • One Bonus Action — Only if a feature grants one (most of you have one — USE IT).
  • One Reaction — Between turns (opportunity attack, Shield, Uncanny Dodge…).
  • One free object interaction — Draw a weapon, open a door.

✦Common actions anyone can take

  • Attack — One attack (more with Extra Attack). Shove & Grapple are options of this action.
  • Dash — Double your movement this turn.
  • Disengage — Your movement won't provoke opportunity attacks this turn.
  • Dodge — Attacks against you have disadvantage; you make Dex saves with advantage (until your next turn). Use it when low or holding ground.
  • Help — Give an ally advantage — on a check, or on an attack against a creature within 5 ft of you.
  • Hide — Stealth check to become hidden (needs cover/obscurement); attacking from hiding has advantage.
  • Ready — Set a trigger now (“when X happens, I do Y”) and spend your reaction to do it.
  • Search — Actively look for something (Perception or Investigation).
  • Shove attack option — Athletics vs their Athletics/Acrobatics: push 5 ft or knock prone.
  • Grapple attack option — Athletics contest: target's Speed becomes 0.
  • Improvise a weapon — Hit with a chair/rock/torch: usually 1d4 + your mod (or use a similar weapon's stats).
  • Use an Object — Pull a lever, drink a potion, throw oil or holy water.

✦⚠ Common mistakes (and the rule behind them)

  • Provoking opportunity attacks — Moving out of an enemy's reach lets it swing for free. Leave melee with Disengage, flight, or Misty Step — never a plain walk.
  • Ranged attacks in melee — Firing a bow / thrown weapon / ranged spell attack while a hostile creature is within 5 ft of you is at DISADVANTAGE. Step away first, or switch to a melee weapon.
  • Catching friends in your spells — Area spells (Ice Knife's burst, Faerie Fire, Entangle, Thunderwave…) hit EVERYONE in the area, allies included. Check the shape and where your friends are first.
  • Double concentration — You can keep only ONE Concentration spell at a time (Bless, Shield of Faith, Flame Blade, Heroism, Faerie Fire…). Taking damage forces a CON save — DC 10 or half the damage, whichever's higher.
  • Forgetting your bonus action & reaction — Half your power lives there. Spend them every round.
Golden rule
Pick a lane and commit. Melee fighters get in and STAY; ranged folk start at the back and never enter melee.

⚔Kree

Rogue 5 · Scout (Aarakocra) — the Skirmisher

Your job
Flit to a safe angle, land ONE big hit, never get surrounded. You can FLY (50 ft) — take the high ground.

Attacks

  • Shortbow +5 · 1d6+2 · 80/320 — From range, not in melee (point-blank = disadvantage).
  • Rapier / Black Sword (Riftshard) +5 · 1d6–1d8+2 · finesse — For diving in beside an ally. The Black Sword works like a shortsword (1d6+2, finesse).
  • Dagger +5 · 1d4+2 — Thrown 20/60.

Sneak Attack — +3d6 (your main damage)

  • Once per turn — Add 3d6 when you hit with a finesse/ranged weapon AND you have advantage, OR an ally is within 5 ft of your target (and you don't have disadvantage). Set this up EVERY turn.

Bonus action — use it every turn

  • Steady Aim Bonus — Advantage on your next attack (your Speed = 0 this turn) → guaranteed Sneak Attack with the bow.
  • Cunning Action Bonus — Dash, Disengage, or Hide. Disengage = leave melee with NO opportunity attack.

Reaction & Gear

  • Uncanny Dodge Reaction — Halve the damage of one attack that hits you.
  • Potion of Healing ×2 Action — Regain 2d4+2 HP. Also Fire-Resistance potion; Red Amulet attuned.

Your race — Aarakocra

  • Flight Speed 50 ft — Not in medium/heavy armor. Stay airborne above melee reach — only fliers & ranged foes can touch you. Dive to strike, climb back up.
  • Talons Unarmed 1d4 slashing — A natural weapon even with your hands full.
✓ A good turn
Steady Aim (bonus) → shoot with advantage → Sneak Attack. OR fly down beside whatever the front line is fighting, stab (ally = Sneak Attack), fly off next turn.
✗ Don’t
Bow someone right next to you (disadvantage) or run off on foot through melee — Cunning Action: Disengage, or fly straight up.

⚔Wilhelm

Artificer 5 — the Toolbox

Your job
Stay at the back, make everyone better, never be in melee. You also build & tinker — lean on it.

Attacks (from range)

  • Flintlock (Repeating Shot) +6 · 1d8+3 — Makes its own ammo; fire from 60+ ft. Not point-blank (disadvantage).
  • Spear (Returning) +6 · 1d6+3 — Throw it; it flies back each turn.
  • Ray of Frost cantrip · Action · 60 ft — Ranged spell atk, 1d8 cold, target's Speed −10 ft.

Spells — buff & control (1st ×4, 2nd ×2)

  • Faerie Fire 1st · Action · Conc 1 min — 20-ft cube, Dex save or outlined → everyone attacks them with advantage. Best opener. (Mind allies in the cube.)
  • Shield 1st · Reaction — When hit / hit by Magic Missile: +5 AC, can turn the hit into a miss.
  • Heroism 1st · Action · Conc 1 min — Target can't be frightened, gains +3 temp HP each turn.
  • Arcane Weapon 1st · Bonus · Conc 1 hr — A weapon gains +1d6 of a chosen energy type and counts as magical.

Tinkering — you make stuff

  • Magical Tinkering — Imbue tiny objects with light, a recorded 6-word message, an odor, or a sound.
  • Infuse Item — Enhanced Weapon (+1), Repeating Shot, Returning Weapon, Replicate Magic Item — Goggles of Night, Pot of Alchemy.
  • The Right Tool for the Job 1 hr — Conjure any artisan's tools. Cinderwarden Coat = fire resistance.
✓ A good turn
Faerie Fire on the enemy clump → whole party hits them with advantage → fire the flintlock. Keep Shield in your pocket.
✗ Don’t
Walk into melee or shoot point-blank. Stay back, buff, and plink.

⚔Lloyd

Paladin 5 · Oath of Devotion (Orc) — the Wall

Your job
Stand in front of Kree & Wilhelm, soak hits, DO NOT run. Anything slipping past you eats your opportunity attack.

Attacks — two per turn (Extra Attack)

  • Shovel/quarterstaff · Longsword +5 · 1d8+2 (longsword 1d10+2 two-handed) — Javelin +5, 1d6+2, thrown. The shovel is canon.

Divine Smite — your burst (DON'T FORGET!)

  • On a melee HIT Spend a slot — +2d8 radiant (1st), +1d8 per higher slot, +1d8 more vs undead/fiends. One free smite/long rest. Hit first, THEN choose to smite.

Heal & Channel Divinity (2/rest)

  • Lay on Hands Action · Touch — 25-HP pool: heal, or 5 to cure a disease/poison.
  • Sacred Weapon Action · 1 min — +4 (CHA) to attacks, sheds light, counts as magical.
  • Turn the Unholy Action — Fiends/undead within 30 ft: Wis save or flee 1 min.

Spells (1st ×4, 2nd ×2)

  • Bless 1st · Action · Conc 1 min — You + 2 allies add 1d4 to attacks AND saves. Superb opener.
  • Shield of Faith 1st · Bonus · Conc — +2 AC to a target (yourself → AC 20).
  • Cure Wounds · Command · Aid · Zone of Truth — Heal 1d8+4 · one-word command (Wis save) · party +5 max HP · 15-ft no-lie zone.

Your race — Orc

  • Adrenaline Rush Bonus · prof/rest — Dash AND gain temp HP = prof bonus. Close fast or buffer a hit.
  • Relentless Endurance 1/long rest — Drop to 1 HP instead of 0 — tank fearlessly.
  • Darkvision 120 · Powerful Build — See far in the dark; count as one size larger for Shove/Grapple & carrying.
✓ A good turn
Bless or Shield of Faith (bonus) → attack twice → smite on a hit. Keep Lay on Hands ready; block the lane to the squishies.
✗ Don’t
Chase fleeing enemies or back off. Plant yourself between the enemy and your friends and HOLD.

⚔Kenra

Druid 5 · Circle of the Moon (Kenku) — the Shapeshifter

Your job
Wild Shape into a beast and tank up front (a second health bar), OR stand back and burn enemies with Flame Blade & Ice Knife. Commit to one.

Wild Shape — tank mode (Bonus, 2/short rest)

  • Become a beast (CR ≤ 1) Bonus — Bear = tough + multiattack; Wolf = knock prone + pack tactics. Beast HP shields yours. (No casting while shaped.)
  • Wild Shape Healing Bonus, while shaped — Spend a slot to heal 1d8 / 2d8 / 3d8.

Spells — caster mode (1st ×4, 2nd ×3, 3rd ×2)

  • Flame Blade 2nd · Bonus · Conc 10 min — A flaming scimitar; Action to attack for 3d6 fire each turn. Single-target — safe near allies.
  • Ice Knife 1st · Action · 60 ft — Ranged spell atk 1d10; then a 2d6 cold burst — everyone within 5 ft of the target (allies too!) makes a Dex save DC 16.
  • Summon Beast 2nd · Action · Conc 1 hr — A bestial spirit that fights for you.
  • Primal Savagery cantrip · Action — Melee spell atk, 1d10 acid (it's an Action, not a bonus action).

Your race — Kenku

  • Mimicry — Reproduce any sound/voice you've heard (Deception/Performance to convince). Throw a voice to misdirect or lure foes.
  • Expert Duplication · Kenku Recall — Advantage to copy writing or craft; an edge when an ally Helps you.
✓ A good turn
Bonus-action Wild Shape into the bear → maul something and stay in their face. OR stay back: Flame Blade up, attack with it each turn, lob Ice Knife at clustered foes (mind allies).
✗ Don’t
Hit-and-run as squishy 38-HP Kenra. Commit as a beast (you've got the HP) or fight from the back.

⚔Thalasstheos

Druid · Circle of the Coast (Water Genasi) — the Tide (NPC, run by the DM)

Your job
Back-line healer & controller. Keep the party standing, lock enemies down, never melee-dash.

Healing & Support

  • Healing Word 1st · Bonus · 60 ft — Heal 1d4+mod at range WITHOUT using your action.
  • Cure Wounds · Goodberry 1st — Heal 1d8+mod (touch) · 10 berries, each 1 HP. Aqua Affinity: drink water to heal + recover slots.

Control & Escape

  • Entangle · Faerie Fire 1st · Conc — Restrain / expose foes in an area (mind allies).
  • Misty Step 2nd · Bonus — Teleport 30 ft — escape WITHOUT provoking. Mirror Image to survive.

Your race — Water Genasi

  • Amphibious · Swim 30 — Breathe air & water; never drowns. Fights freely in/under water.
  • Acid Resistance · Shape Water — Resists acid; shapes & creates water to control the battlefield.
✓ A good turn
Heal whoever's lowest, drop control on the enemies, Misty Step out instead of walking.
✗ Don’t
Wade into melee. She's the lifeline — keep her back and mobile.
